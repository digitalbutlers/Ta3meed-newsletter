Ta3meed Finance partnership program

Supply Chain Finance An Alternative Way to Finance

The Supply Chain Finance (SCF) Program at Ta3meed has been developed by Ta3meed especially for selected Ta3meed partners. As a Fintech solution, the Supply Chain Finance Program at Ta3meed is a cost-efficient way for ta3meed partners to convert 100% of the value Of the purchase orders they received into non-recourse cash at an acceptable discount rate

The Supply Chain Finance Program helps Ta3meed partners improve the cash flow and optimizes their working capital. The relationship between Ta3meed and its partners can become even more efficient with the utilization of the SCF Program.

Ta3meed Platform is responsible for the introduction of the Supply Chain Finance Program and the coordination between involved partners

Ta3meed web-Mobile App platform offers an efficient and user-friendly interface for Ta3meed partners

Ta3meed is licensed by SAMA* and is the leading Fintech in the field of Purchase order and Supply chain finance solutions, to provide a platform for funding management.

*Ta3meed is a peer-peer fintech licensed by Saudi Central Bank (SAMA)

Summary of Partner Benefits

Cash Flow Improvement

Get paid quickly: Three days following the acceptance of your client's application by Ta3meed. Application process is fully automated through Ta3meed platform

Working Capital Optimization

Purchase order & SCF financing is a non-recourse cash that improves liquidity, reduces Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) and improves the debt/equity ratio

Cost Reduction

Get paid automatically on purchase orders once they are approved, and reduce Account Receivable & collection costs relative to your receivables

Finance The SCF Process

The Ta3meed Supply Chain Finance Program enables selected Ta3meed partners to improve their liquidity through the financing of their received purchase orders at very acceptable rates.

I need finance for clients:

Partner invites his client to register and get financing from Ta3meed Platform

Clients register and apply for PO financing from Ta3meed

Partner get paid early and receive a non-recourse cash from Ta3meed Platform

Partner delivers goods and issues invoices to his client in exactlythe same way priorto enrollment

Client pays Ta3meed at the agreed maturity date

Summary of Partner Benefits

The only cost of the program is the discount charge that a partner makes to receive early payment. There are no fees to join, maintain, or exit Ta3meed partners program
